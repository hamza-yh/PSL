What is the PSL?

The PSL Tournament is a double-elimination bracket where you can compete for cash prizes and the title of PSL Champion. Here, we also provide meet-and-greets with your favorite PSL athletes, as well as services such as vending and coaching sessions.

Results will be tracked with state-of-the-art technology and our proprietary ranking system. Once the bracket has been reduced to the top 8, We will provide a 3-hour, high quality broadcast for each event, that anyone can enjoy from home.
