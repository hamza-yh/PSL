What is the PSL?

PSL Qualifiers are the WCA Competitions in which you compete to get the fastest 3x3 Average of 5 possible. Based on your performance, you can qualify for our PSL Invitationals held on the same day. Here, we also provide meet-and-greets with your favorite PSL athletes, as well as services such as vending and coaching sessions. PSL Qualifier Competitors are automatically granted a spectator ticket to the invitational event!

PSL Invitationals are our double-elimination tournaments with new rules and regulations where people compete for cash prizes and the title of PSL Champion. Results will be tracked via our proprietary ranking system. We will provide a 3-hour, high quality broadcast for each invitiational event.
