PSL - Berkeley 2025

The first ever Premier Speedcubing League event! Located in what many consider to be the birthplace of modern speedcubing, 64 competitors will battle to find out who will be the inaugural winner of the PSL!

Registration Info

  • Date: March 16th, 2025
  • Location: Pauley Ballroom, UC Berkeley, Berkeley, California
  • Prize Pool: 1st: $500,   2nd: $250,   3rd: $100,   4th: $80,   Tied 5th: $35

  • Visit the PSL Berkeley Page on start.gg for more information and to register for the event!
