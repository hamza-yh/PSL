Welcome to the League

The Premier Speedcubing League is home to the highest standard of competitive speedcubers and cubing entertainment. Here, you’ll see some of the greatest solves recorded on the rubik’s cube, coupled with our state-of-the-art innovation in statistics, hardware, software, broadcasting, and content creation.

We are featuring 6 in-person invitational events that are a part of our “PSL Tour”. Capping off our competitive season will be the “PSL Championship” where we crown our Grand Champion, the winner of the league and a coveted cash prize of $1000 per local event.

Every season, we will be featuring our partners and athletes. This season, look out for Dylan Miller, and Matty Hiroto Inaba as official athletes of the PSL. Who will you be rooting for, and which free agent will be the next one to step up to the plate?
