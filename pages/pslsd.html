PSL - San Diego 2025

PSL San Diego will be our second PSL Competetion! Here, we will have a 64-person double-elimination tournament bracket where competitors battle each other one on one. Then, the top 8 competitors will battle on stage head to head to decide who will take home the title of PSL Champion.

Registration Info

  • Date: March 22nd, 2025
  • Location: Price Center West Ballroom, UC San Diego, San Diego, California

  • Visit the PSL San Diego page on start.gg for more information and to register for the event!
