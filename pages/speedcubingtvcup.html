The Speedcubing.tv Cup

We are proud to be a partner of The Speedcubing.tv Cup. The winner of this online tournament will be granted a free ticket to entry for an upcoming Premier Speedcubing League event.

This online tournament serves as a training ground for competitors, and is also a place for the league to test and implement innovations in format and regulations.

To find out more about this opportunity, and whether you are eligible, check out Speedcubing.tv!
