2/24/25 - Cubing Life Academy Partners with the PSL

Hey everyone,

The PSL is excited to announce that we are offering attendees discounts for coaching services from Cubing Life Academy for season 1. If you pay for a regular PSL ticket, they will provide $25 off your next coaching session. If you pay for a VIP PSL ticket, then they will provide $50 off. We hope this presents a great opportunity for people to have improved targeted practice if their goal is to continuously get better!

2/15/25 - PSL Theme Song

Hey everyone,

We are excited to announce that “SKATE” by DG the Artist is the official theme song of the PSL for Season 1. We believe this to be a part of our overall direction of a league being made by cubers, for cubers.

His song perfectly encapsulates the epic energy we aim to achieve here in the PSL. If you’ve purchased tickets, be ready to jam out with us in-person on March 16th! If you cannot, you can party with us virtually by tuning into the broadcasts on SpeedcubingTV.
